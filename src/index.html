Also, did you know that ...

... this website uses no JavaScript at all? It's built entirely in static, lean and mean HTML and CSS. You often don't need JavaScript. Despite I consider myself an expert at React (I even dare to say I love it), and frequently use other JavaScript libraries and frameworks all the time, what renders in your user's browser is ultimately what counts! JavaScript shouldn't be used for the sake of it. Use it wisely, to help users interact with your product in a more natural way.
